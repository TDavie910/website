About us

Tyla’s Pet Shop is your neighborhood destination for happy, healthy pets and the people who love them. We offer a carefully selected range of premium pet foods, tasty treats, durable toys, cozy bedding, and essential supplies for dogs, cats, small animals, birds, and more. At Tyla’s Pet Shop, we believe every pet deserves personalized care. Our friendly, knowledgeable team is always ready to help you choose the right nutrition, find the perfect toy, or get advice on grooming and everyday care. We focus on quality products, trusted brands, and a welcoming atmosphere where pets are just as valued as their owners. Whether you’re a first-time pet parent or a lifelong animal lover, Tyla’s Pet Shop is here to support you every step of the way — because pets aren’t just animals, they’re family.
